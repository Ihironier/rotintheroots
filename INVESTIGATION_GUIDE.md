# Investigation Guide

## Steps to Conducting an Investigation
1. **Establish the Mystery**  
   Define the problem or crime that needs solving.
2. **Gather Evidence**  
   Determine what clues or items the players can find.
3. **Interview NPCs**  
   Create key NPCs who have information relevant to the investigation.
4. **Connect the Dots**  
   Encourage players to think critically and make connections between clues.
5. **Present the Conclusion**  
   Allow players to present their findings and solve the mystery.

## Key NPCs for Investigations
- Detective Eldrin: A meticulous investigator with a keen eye for detail.
- Ms. Lyra: The librarian who knows the town's secrets.
- Old Man Radley: A witness who saw something unusual during the night.
