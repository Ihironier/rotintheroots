# Location Descriptions

## The Enchanted Forest
A dense, mysterious forest filled with ancient trees and whispers of magic. Sounds of wildlife echo through the shadows, and secrets await in every corner.

## Abandoned Mine
Once a bustling site, now just a skeleton of its former self. Dark and foreboding, it holds countless treasures and dangers within.

## The Town of Oakhaven
A quaint village known for its friendly residents and dark secrets. The cobblestone streets are lined with old, creaking houses.
