# Campaign Overview

## Campaign Premise
The campaign centers around the mysterious phenomenon known as "The Rot" that has begun to infect the small town of Oakhaven, Ohio, in the year 2026. The players must unravel the origins of The Rot, discover its connection to dark forces, and confront the impending doom threatening their world.

## Setting
- **Location**: Oakhaven, Ohio 2026  
- **Description**: A once thriving town now shrouded in darkness and dread, Oakhaven is marked by decay and an underlying sense of despair as the strange epidemic spreads.

## Central Conflict
The Rot, an otherworldly plague, is infecting the residents and the land of Oakhaven, leading to horror and madness. As tensions rise, the players must confront the mysteries behind this affliction and the dark forces at work. 

## Full 4-Act Campaign Arc
### Act 1: The Unraveling (Sessions 1-3)
- **Session 1**: Introduction to Oakhaven, initial encounters with The Rot, and discovery of eerie symptoms in townsfolk.
- **Session 2**: Investigation of reports and sightings. Players meet NPCs and discover the growing danger.
- **Session 3**: Major revelations about The Rot's origins and its connection to a local legend or artifact.

### Act 2: Into the Dark (Sessions 4-6)
- **Session 4**: Exploring dark locations and places affected by The Rot. First encounters with corrupted NPCs.
- **Session 5**: Players gain a clue to a powerful artifact that can combat The Rot but at a cost.
- **Session 6**: Final confrontation with a key antagonist who reveals the importance of the artifact.

### Act 3: The Upside Down (Sessions 7-8)
- **Session 7**: Players venture into a warped reality created by The Rot, facing their greatest fears.
- **Session 8**: Showdown with The Rot’s true form and a chance to save or further doom Oakhaven. 

#### Consequences
- **Victory**: The Rot is purged. Oakhaven begins to heal, and NPCs return to normal.
- **Failure**: The Rot spreads beyond Oakhaven, leading to worldwide consequences and chaos.

## Key Themes
- The nature of corruption and purity  
- Courage in the face of despair  
- The interconnectedness of fate  

## Tone and Atmosphere
- **Overall Tone**: Dark, mysterious, and suspenseful.  
- **Atmosphere**: Heavy with a sense of foreboding, filled with themes of dread and mystery.

## Pre-Campaign Timeline of Events (1983-2026)
- **1983**: Initial disappearance of townsfolk leading to urban myths.
- **1995**: Local legends of a cursed artifact linked to The Rot begin to circulate.
- **2000**: Sudden increase in supernatural occurrences around Oakhaven.
- **2010-2020**: Small disasters hint at a larger issue, ignored by authorities.
- **2026**: The Rot becomes manifest, leading to widespread chaos in Oakhaven.

## Important NPCs and Factions
- **Mayor Robinson**: A conflicted leader trying to keep order while dealing with The Rot.
- **Dr. Elaine Foster**: A local doctor striving to understand the affliction.
- **The Cult of the Rot**: A faction that worships the mystery behind The Rot and seeks to harness its power.

## What Players Need to Know at Start
- They are residents of Oakhaven and witnesses to the onset of The Rot.
- The importance of various NPCs and factions in the town's survival.

## What Players Should Not Know Yet
- The true nature of The Rot and the implications of their actions.
- The connections between events in Oakhaven and past incidents from 1983 onward.
