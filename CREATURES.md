# Encounter Creatures Stat Blocks

## The Theselhydra
- **Type:** Huge Aberration  
- **Armor Class:** 16  
- **Hit Points:** 187  

### Abilities:
- **Multiple Heads:** The Theselhydra has five heads. For each head, it can make an additional bite attack during its turn.
- **Regeneration:** The Theselhydra regains 10 hit points at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 hit point.
- **Bioluminescent Pulse:** As a bonus action, the Theselhydra can emit a glowing pulse, illuminating a 60-foot radius and giving creatures disadvantage on attacks against it until the end of its next turn.

### Actions:
- **Multiattack:** The Theselhydra makes five attacks: three with its bite and two with its tentacle whip.
- **Bite:** Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (2d10 + 7) piercing damage.
- **Tentacle Whip:** Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d8 + 6) bludgeoning damage and the target is grappled (escape DC 17).

### Lair Actions:
On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), the Theselhydra can take one of the following lair actions:
1. Animate the bioluminescent flora to alert it of intruders.
2. Create difficult terrain in its lair.

### Lore:
The Theselhydra dwells in murky waters, using its multiple heads to attack unsuspecting prey.

## Demogorgon
- **Type:** Large Aberration  
- **Armor Class:** 17  
- **Hit Points:** 150  
- **Challenge Rating:** 18  

### Abilities:
- **Legendary Resistance:** If the Demogorgon fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Aberrant Mind:** It can cast spells that manipulate the thoughts and minds of its foes.

### Actions:
- **Multiattack:** The Demogorgon can make three attacks: one with its bite and two with its tusks.
- **Bite:** Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 30 (4d10 + 7) piercing damage.
- **Tentacle:** Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 24 (4d6 + 7) bludgeoning damage and the target must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened.

### Lore:
Demogorgon, the prince of demons, constantly schemes for power and control in the chaotic realms of the Abyss.

## Vine Creature
- **Type:** Medium Plant  
- **Armor Class:** 13  
- **Hit Points:** 22  
- **Challenge Rating:** 2  

### Abilities:
- **Acidic Sap:** The Vine Creature's attacks deal additional acid damage.
- **Root Bond:** It can bond with the earth to heal itself.

### Actions:
- **Multiattack:** The Vine Creature makes two whip attacks.
- **Whip:** Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage and 4 (1d8) acid damage.

### Lore:
Vine Creatures are guardians of nature, ensnaring unwelcome visitors with their grasping vines.

## Corrupted Deer
- **Type:** Large Beast/Aberration  
- **Armor Class:** 13  
- **Hit Points:** 27  
- **Challenge Rating:** 2  

### Abilities:
- **Pack Tactics:** The Corrupted Deer has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the deer's allies is within 5 ft. of the creature.

### Actions:
- **Multiattack:** The Corrupted Deer makes two attacks: one with its Antlers and one with its Hoof.
- **Antlers:** Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage.
- **Hoof:** Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d10 + 3) bludgeoning damage.

### Lore:
The Corrupted Deer is a creature twisted by dark magic, leading packs of aberrations in the deep woods.

## Shadow Swarm
- **Type:** Tiny Swarm  
- **Armor Class:** 14  
- **Hit Points:** 11  
- **Challenge Rating:** 1/8  

### Abilities:
- **Swarm Mechanics:** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa. It can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.

### Actions:
- **Multiattack:** The Shadow Swarm makes two swarm attacks.
- **Swarm:** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, one target in its space. Hit: 7 (2d6) necrotic damage.

### Lore:
Shadow swarms haunt the night, consuming the light and vitality of their surroundings.

## Black Goo
- **Type:** Environmental Hazard  
- **AC:** 10  
- **HP:** -  

### Mechanics:
When a creature enters the area of the black goo, it must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 1d10 acid damage.

### Lore:
The black goo is a malevolent substance that corrupts everything it touches, creating abominations.

## The Rot Source
- **Type:** Colossal Aberration Entity  
- **Armor Class:** 22  
- **Hit Points:** 500  

### Abilities:
- **Corruption Aura:** Creatures within 100 feet of the Rot Source must make a Constitution saving throw at the start of their turn, taking 3d6 necrotic damage on a failure.

### Actions:
- **Multiattack:** The Rot Source can make three attacks: one with its slam and two with its spore burst.
- **Slam:** Melee Weapon Attack: +15 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 48 (6d10 + 12) bludgeoning damage.
- **Spore Burst:** Ranged Attack: +12 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (4d10) necrotic damage and the target is poisoned.

### Lore:
The Rot Source represents the ultimate decay, an entity that corrupts everything around it and spreads its influence.
