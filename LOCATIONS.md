# LOCATIONS.md

## Oakhaven, Ohio
### Overview and Geography
Oakhaven is a serene town nestled in the heart of Ohio, characterized by rolling hills and dense woodlands. It has a rich history and a close-knit community that thrives on local events, farmers' markets, and a sense of togetherness. The town's geography includes the winding Millbrook Creek, which cuts through the area, and vast forests that provide both adventure and danger.

## Oakhaven High School
### Main Building
The main building stands tall at the center of the campus, constructed with brick and large glass windows, offering a glimpse into the daily activities of students.

### Football Field and Bleachers
The football field is a hub of excitement during fall, with home games drawing crowds. The bleachers are often filled with enthusiastic supporters.

### Woods Access
A worn path behind the school leads to the woods, often taken by adventurous students seeking a thrill.

### Parking
The parking lot is spacious, usually filled with teachers' cars during the day and dotted with students' vehicles after hours.

### Basement
The basement holds many secrets; it is dark, damp, and rumored to be a meeting place for local students planning escapades.

## The Woods Behind the School
### Environmental Hazards
This area is littered with micro-environmental hazards, including contaminated water sources and strange fungi.

### Creatures
A variety of creatures inhabit the woods, from harmless birds to menacing creatures that wander at night.

### Dead Zone Description
Certain areas are marked as dead zones, with heightened electromagnetic activity causing disorientation and distress.

### Portal Location
A hidden portal exists, often described by locals as a flickering light among the trees, leading to unknown realms.

### Creek Bed
The creek bed runs through the woods, its clear waters disguising the dangers lurking beneath.

### Ritual Circle
A clearing marked by ancient stones, this ritual circle is used in local folklore and rumored magic.

## Millbrook Creek
### Current State
Millbrook Creek is currently marred by pollution, with discolored waters and dead fish seen along the banks.

### Contamination Levels
High contamination levels due to industrial runoff have disrupted the ecosystem, making it unsafe for local wildlife.

### Theselhydra Lair Location
The Theselhydra, a formidable creature, makes its lair in a deep bend of the creek, protected by its murky depths.

## Nexus Research Facility
### Exterior
The facility has a stark, sterile appearance that intimidates visitors, surrounded by barbed wire and armed guards.

### Interior Labs
Inside, aggressively lit labs cluttered with equipment showcase ongoing experiments, with ominous electrical sounds echoing.

### Detention Areas
Adjoining the labs, detention areas house those who threaten the facility's interests, shrouded in mystery and fear.

### Gate Control Room
This nerve center is responsible for all entrances and is a guarded secret, rarely seen from the outside.

### Security
State-of-the-art security systems line the facility, intended to deter both insider threats and external breaches.

## Downtown Oakhaven
### Diner
A popular local diner where townspeople gather, famous for its pies and gossip.

### Police Station
The central hub for law enforcement, known for being more of a community help center than a traditional police station.

### Town Hall
The town hall hosts community meetings and is decorated with artifacts showcasing Oakhaven's rich history.

### Library
A quiet sanctuary filled with books, offering both a retreat and a research hub for locals and visitors alike.

### Hospital
The local hospital, while small, is equipped to handle emergencies but often stretches its resources during crises.

### Gas Station
The gas station is a local landmark, offering fuel and a few essentials; it's known for the quirky conversations among its patrons.

## The Old Mill
### Description
The old mill stands as a testament to Oakhaven's industrial past, now a partially renovated structure.

### History
Once a bustling center for grain production, it now holds remnants of its past, whispering stories of old.

### Current Use
The mill is used for occasional town events and artisan markets, bridging the past with the present.

### Underground Tunnels
Rumors suggest intricate underground tunnels, leading to various locations in town, some still unexplored.

## The Upside Down
### General Description
The Upside Down is a dark mirror of Oakhaven, filled with distorted versions of familiar places and lurking dangers.

### Inverted Locations
Notable landmarks, like the high school and the diner, appear in twisted forms, engulfed in shadows and mystery.

### Gate Chamber
The Gate Chamber is the threshold between realities, pulsing with an uneasy energy, marking the boundary where dangers seep into the known world.
