# ENCOUNTERS

## Session Breakdown  
1. **Session 1: The Call to Adventure**  
   - **Key Scenes:**  
     - Players receive a quest from a local lord.  
     - A storm arises, hinting at trouble ahead.  
   - **Combat Encounters:**  
     - Bandit ambush (Difficulty: Easy)  
       - **Stat Block:**  
         - Bandit: AC 13, HP 11, Attack: shortsword +5 (1d6+2)

2. **Session 2: Into the Woods**  
   - **Key Scenes:**  
     - Traveling through a cursed forest.  
   - **Combat Encounters:**  
     - Swarm of wolves (Difficulty: Medium)  
       - **Stat Block:**  
         - Wolf: AC 13, HP 11, Attack: bite +4 (1d6+2)

3. **Session 3: The Haunted Ruins**  
   - **Key Scenes:**  
     - Exploring an ancient ruin filled with spirits.  
   - **Combat Encounters:**  
     - Ghosts of fallen warriors (Difficulty: Hard)  
       - **Stat Block:**  
         - Ghost: AC 11, HP 30, Attack: scare +5 (2d6)

4. **Session 4: The Dark Cult**  
   - **Key Scenes:**  
     - Discovering a cult performing dark rituals.  
   - **Combat Encounters:**  
     - Cultists (Difficulty: Medium)  
       - **Stat Block:**  
         - Cultist: AC 12, HP 9, Attack: dagger +4 (1d4+2)

5. **Session 5: The Siege**  
   - **Key Scenes:**  
     - Fending off a siege on a town.  
   - **Combat Encounters:**  
     - Siege weapons and enemy soldiers (Difficulty: Hard)  
       - **Stat Block:**  
         - Soldier: AC 15, HP 20, Attack: spear +5 (1d8+3)

6. **Session 6: The Betrayal**  
   - **Key Scenes:**  
     - Dealing with a traitor in the party.  
   - **Combat Encounters:**  
     - The traitor and their mercenaries (Difficulty: Very Hard)  
       - **Stat Block:**  
         - Mercenary: AC 14, HP 25, Attack: sword +6 (1d8+3)

7. **Session 7: The Underworld**  
   - **Key Scenes:**  
     - Descending into a dark cavern.  
   - **Combat Encounters:**  
     - Undead minions (Difficulty: Medium)  
       - **Stat Block:**  
         - Zombie: AC 8, HP 12, Attack: slam +3 (1d6)

8. **Session 8: The Final Showdown**  
   - **Key Scenes:**  
     - Confronting the main antagonist.  
   - **Combat Encounters:**  
     - Boss fight with the antagonist (Difficulty: Deadly)  
       - **Stat Block:**  
         - Antagonist: AC 16, HP 50, Attack: fireball +8 (3d8)

9. **Session 9: The Aftermath**   
   - **Key Scenes:**  
     - Resolving the consequences of their actions.  
   - **Skill Challenges:**  
     - Rebuilding the town, helping the citizens.

10. **Session 10: A New Beginning**  
    - **Key Scenes:**  
      - A hint of new adventures on the horizon.
    - **Scaling Guidance:**  
      - Adjust enemies for party levels 1-10 as needed.

## Scaling Guidance  
- For levels 1-3: Lower the HP and damage of enemies.  
- For levels 4-6: Use standard enemy stats.  
- For levels 7-10: Increase the HP and abilities of enemies.  

## Skill Challenges Overview  
- Challenges should be adjusted based on the party’s strengths. Consider implementing skill checks for Diplomacy, Stealth, and Investigation when appropriate.
