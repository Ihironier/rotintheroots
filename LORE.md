# LORE

## Upside Down Origins
The Upside Down is a dark parallel dimension to our own, originating from an ancient cataclysmic event that tore through the fabric of reality, creating a rift that allowed dark energies to seep through. This realm is a reflection of our world, twisted and corrupted, filled with grotesque versions of its flora and fauna.

## Nature of the Rot
The Rot is an insidious force that corrupts everything it touches. It is a manifestation of the Upside Down's energy infiltrating our world, spreading decay and chaos. Those affected by the Rot often succumb to madness, transforming into monstrous forms that serve the dark will of the Upside Down.

## Government's Role and Plan
The government acknowledges the existence of the Upside Down but keeps it a closely guarded secret. They have plans to harness the Rot's power for their own ends, conducting experiments to understand its properties. However, their machinations only seem to exacerbate the problem, creating more chaos as they attempt to control the uncontrollable.

## 1980s Connection
The events of the 1980s marked a significant escalation in disturbances related to the Upside Down. Several missing person cases and strange occurrences in towns across America can be traced back to that era, as the fabric between the dimensions began to thin. This period serves as the starting point for many character arcs and plotlines revolving around the mystery of the Upside Down.

## Information About the Black Goo
The black goo is a sentient substance that acts as a conduit for the Rot. It moves with a will of its own, capable of sensing and seeking out beings of power or those with a connection to the Upside Down. Those who come into contact with it often find themselves influenced or corrupted.

## Creature Hierarchy
The creatures of the Upside Down operate under a strict hierarchy, with the Mind Flayer at the top, commanding legions of smaller entities such as Demogorgons and other aberrant beings. Each creature has its role in maintaining the balance (or imbalance) within the realm and exerting influence over the human world.

## Gate Mechanics
Gates are the physical manifestations between the Upside Down and our world. They appear as portals, often in locations of emotional trauma or significant events. Activating a gate generally requires a powerful emotional connection or a person who has been influenced by the Rot.

## Sealing Options
Sealing a gate requires immense concentration and often sacrifices, as it involves binding the energies of both realms. Various methods have been attempted, from powerful rituals to technological enhancements, but few have succeeded without severe consequences.

## Character Secrets
Many characters have hidden pasts linked to the Upside Down—secrets that could change their lives and the lives of those around them. These include connections to previous encounters with the Rot, family members lost to the chaos, or hidden powers born from the corruption of the Upside Down.

## Possible Endings
The story can unfold in several ways, depending on character choices and actions throughout. A possible ending could see the gates sealed but at a great personal cost, while another could involve an irreversible merging of the two worlds, leading to widespread devastation.

## Mysteries that May Never Be Solved
Numerous enigmas linger, including the true nature of the Upside Down, the origins of the Rot, and the ultimate fate of those lost within its depths. Some questions may remain unanswered, leaving both characters and players pondering long after the story concludes.
