# Characters: The Rot in the Roots

> All characters exist in **Oakhaven, Ohio, 2026**. No fantasy trappings.

---

## Player Characters

### Fyndor Kasparek
**Role:** The Tech Obsessive / Scanner Kid
**Age:** 16
**Background:** Fyndor has had a police scanner since he was 12. He knows the frequencies for every emergency service in the county, maintains a shortwave radio in his room, and has a YouTube channel about urban exploration with 847 subscribers. He first heard "VOSS-7" on the scanner three weeks before Marcus disappeared — and didn't know what it meant until now.

**Player Hook:** Fyndor recorded the scanner burst the night Marcus vanished. He is the only person with that audio. He doesn't know yet that Nexus Research knows he has it.

**Suggested Class:** Artificer (Artillerist) / Ranger (Gloom Stalker) / Rogue (Arcane Trickster)
**Key Items:** Police scanner, shortwave radio, UV flashlight, soldering kit

**Character Secret (optional reveal):** Fyndor's father worked for Nexus Research in 2018 as a "water quality contractor." He doesn't know what his dad actually did there. His dad left town shortly after and hasn't been back.

---

### Celeste Moreau
**Role:** The One Who Remembers / Creek Kid
**Age:** 17
**Background:** Celeste grew up near Millbrook Creek. As a child she had recurring dreams of something vast and dark in the water — five points of light in the deep, and a sound like breathing through stone. She told a therapist. The therapist moved her on. She told Marcus Chen, who was her best friend. Marcus believed her.

**Player Hook:** When Marcus disappeared, Celeste's creek dreams came back. On the second night, she woke up with her window open and a smear of black goo on the sill. Something visited her. She doesn't know if it was a warning or a greeting.

**Suggested Class:** Druid (Circle of Spores) / Ranger (Horizon Walker) / Warlock (Great Old One)
**Key Items:** Dream journal, pressed creek flowers, a broken compass that started working again recently

**Character Secret (optional reveal):** Celeste's grandmother disappeared in 1987 — one of the three original missing persons when the gate first opened. No one in her family talks about it. The Theselhydra has been encountered in Celeste's dreams in a form she doesn't yet recognize.

### Additional Player Character Hooks (for 3–5 person party)

**The Younger Sibling** (14–15)
Marcus's younger sibling or Fyndor's. Has something of Marcus's that he left behind — a cassette tape labeled "HOME" that he'd been working on. The tape contains a distress signal buried under music. They haven't realized it yet.
*Suggested Class:* Bard / Rogue

**The Athlete** (17–18)
On the football team, had a fight with Marcus the week before he vanished. Has guilt. Also noticed that Coach Hendricks has been acting strange since the summer — and two team members quit with no explanation.
*Suggested Class:* Fighter / Barbarian

**The Transfer Student** (16–17)
Just moved to Oakhaven in September. Doesn't know anyone — which means they see the town clearly, without the filter of familiarity. They've already noticed the dead zone behind the school. Nobody else acts like it's unusual.
*Suggested Class:* Wizard / Artificer

## Key NPCs

### Marcus Chen (Missing)
**Age:** 17
**Description:** Quiet kid. Chess club, after-school D&D club (he was the DM). He is the one who named the Theselhydra — from a Greek root meaning "water serpent." He wrote it in his campaign notes six months before anyone else knew what it was.

**Status:** Alive in the Upside Down. Surviving in the Theselhydra's territory. He has built a shelter of sorts from the ruined mirror-version of his school locker. His cassette player still works — barely. The tape has about 30 minutes of runtime left.

**What Marcus Knows:** Everything, essentially. He has been in the Upside Down for days. He has mapped a significant portion of the mirror-Oakhaven. He knows the Theselhydra is not trying to kill him. He has figured out the lights-communication method. He is terrified but functional.

**What Marcus Needs:** A way home. The gate has to be reachable from both sides simultaneously for extraction.

---

### Dr. Helena Voss
**Role:** Lead Researcher, Nexus Research Oakhaven Site
**Age:** 52
**Appearance:** Conservative clothes, silver hair pulled back, always calm, always a fraction too calm.

**Goal:** Dr. Voss believes the Upside Down is a resource — biological, energetic, possibly pharmaceutical. She is not a monster. She genuinely believes that what she is doing will benefit humanity. This makes her more dangerous, not less.

**What She Knows:** Everything about the gate's history. She knows about the 1987 incident. She knows Marcus fell through. She has classified him as "a civilian exposure event" and is managing containment, which includes keeping the sheriff quiet and monitoring the PCs.

**What She Doesn't Know:** The Rot is not a resource. It is a spreading infection that doesn't negotiate. Her "controlled aperture expansion" has made things significantly worse. The Theselhydra's bond has fractured because she pushed too far.

**Motivation Arc:** Voss can be reasoned with if the PCs can prove that her operation is making the Rot worse. She is not loyal to the institution over the science — if the data shows she is catastrophically wrong, she will shift. This is a potential alliance of desperation in the finale.

**Key Scenes:** Session 5 (reveal), Session 6 (confrontation), Session 8 (possible ally or obstacle)

---

### Sheriff Dale Blackwell
**Role:** Oakhaven Sheriff
**Age:** 58
**Appearance:** Big, tired, too many years in this town. Drives a cruiser that needs new shocks.

**What He Knows:** More than he should. In 2003 he was a deputy when a hiker went missing in the same woods. The case was closed by federal direction. He was told to drop it. He dropped it. He's been dropping things ever since.

**Attitude Toward PCs:** He wants to help. He is afraid of what helping would cost. If the PCs give him evidence that cannot be ignored — something on video, a witness, a sample — he will act. If they just come to him with stories, he will ask them to go home.

**Potential:** Sheriff Blackwell is not compromised. He is cowardly. Those are different things, and the PCs' willingness to treat them as different things determines whether he becomes an ally in the finale.

---

### Deputy Elena Reyes
**Role:** Oakhaven Sheriff's Deputy (new)
**Age:** 29
**Appearance:** Sharp, skeptical in a good way, from out of county.

**Status:** The only law enforcement character who hasn't been conditioned to look away. She's been in Oakhaven for four months. She is already suspicious of the sheriff's "let the feds handle it" policy.

**Role in Campaign:** Ally. She won't arrest the PCs for breaking into places if they can show her why they were there. She can run plates, access missing-person databases, and — crucially — she has a service weapon and will back the PCs up in a crisis.

---

### Margaret Thorne
**Role:** Oakhaven Public Library Director
**Age:** 64
**Appearance:** Reading glasses on a chain, sensible shoes, knows where every book in that building is.

**Role in Campaign:** Information hub. She has been keeping a file of newspaper clippings, county records, and odd notes since the 1987 incident. She doesn't know what she has. The PCs will.

**What She Can Provide:** Historical records, local maps (including pre-1990 creek surveys), the 1987 newspaper articles about the "animal attacks," property records for the Old Mill, and a listening ear. She will not go into the woods with anyone, but she will believe things she can read.

---

### Coach Ray Hendricks
**Role:** Oakhaven High Football Coach
**Age:** 45
**Status:** Compromised by Rot exposure (partial)

**Signs Something is Wrong:** He's been eating less. He smiles at the wrong moments. Three players quit the team over the summer and won't say why. He keeps a padlocked storage room in the field house that smells like copper and wet earth.

**What He Actually Is:** Not fully converted. He was exposed to a minor Rot breach behind the practice field in July. The Rot has a partial hold — enough to cause behavioral drift and to make him protective of the nearby breach site. A Detect Poison/Disease spell (or equivalent) shows something wrong. He can potentially be helped if the breach near the field house is sealed early.

---

### The Chen Family
- **James Chen (Marcus's father):** Working every contact he has. Former engineer. Building a map of Marcus's known routes. Will share it with anyone who takes his son seriously.
- **Linda Chen (Marcus's mother):** Has a social media campaign going. Nexus Research has had it suppressed twice. She doesn't know that yet. She is furious in the way that produces results.
